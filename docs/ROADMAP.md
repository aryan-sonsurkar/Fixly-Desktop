# Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Current)
- ✅ Monorepo setup and architecture
- ✅ Authentication system (email + Google login)
- ✅ Dashboard with daily briefing
- ✅ Assignment management (CRUD, priorities, due dates)
- ✅ AI Assistant (Ollama + Gemini routing)
- ✅ Email intelligence (Gmail OAuth, auto-detection, classification, daily briefing)
- ✅ File upload and attachment management

## Phase 2 — Productivity
- ✅ Pomodoro timer with focus/break cycles
- ⬜ Centralized notification center
- ✅ Analytics dashboard (study time, XP, streaks, calendar heatmap)
- ✅ Daily briefing system
- ⬜ Focus music and ambient sounds

## Phase 3 — AI Expansion
- ✅ PDF intelligence (summarization, explanation, question answering)
- ✅ OCR for handwritten notes and screenshots
- ⬜ AI notes generation from lectures
- ⬜ Study assistant with spaced repetition
- ⬜ Quiz generation from study materials

## Phase 4 — Infrastructure
- ⬜ Auto updater (GitHub Releases)
- ⬜ Crash reporting and error tracking
- ⬜ Performance optimization
- ✅ CI/CD pipeline completion
- ⬜ Icon and branding assets

## Phase 5 — Platform Expansion
- Web application (React + same shared packages)
- Android mobile application
- iOS mobile application

## Phase 6 — Collaboration
- Google Classroom integration
- Moodle and LMS integrations
- WhatsApp study reminders
- Google Calendar sync
- Shared workspaces and collaboration
